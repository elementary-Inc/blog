- Scope of the feature/screenshots
- Ideal/"most apps"
  - Opt in/bind
  - Custom styles
- Other situations
  - Temporary override (like Granite Demo or Ephemeral)
  - Persistent override/opt out (turning off "Follow system style")
  - Multiple color schemes (like Code or Terminal but with multiple user-selectable schemes)
