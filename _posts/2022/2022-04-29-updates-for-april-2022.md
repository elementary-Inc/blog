## What we've been up to

Updates pushed to OS 6

Getting OS 7 ready for launch

## What to Expect

OS 7 will be code named Horus

Will not be making the jump to Wayland this cycle. Many of the gears are turning, but want to prioritize releasing OS 7 quickly.

Creature comforts like Automatic App Updates and Power Profiles

A new, minimal Music

Some things migrated to Gtk4, but not all.

Overall, the feature set difference between 6.1 and 7 will be small at first. However, since we release updates throughout the lifecycle of the OS, expect more features to be added as the year goes on.

Working prototype for offline release upgrades. Once 7 is released, we'll be able to more thoroughly test this and hopefully release the feature quickly for OS 6.

OS 6 will continue to receive updates for Flatpak apps including our default apps and AppCenter apps. Some components may continue to receive a trickle of updates, but development focus is on OS 7. OS 6 should now be considered in maintanence mode; don't expect new features or major fixes.

Lots more details in the release post
